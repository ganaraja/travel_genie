## Visa Requirements

### For USA Citizens

- **Type**: E-visa (Electronic Visa)
- **Processing Time**: 2-4 business days
- **Cost**: $25-100 (depending on duration)
- **Validity**: 30 days to 1 year
- **Application**: Online at https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/

### For UK Citizens

- **Type**: E-visa
- **Processing Time**: 2-4 business days
- **Cost**: £25-100
- **Validity**: 30 days to 1 year

### For India Citizens

- **Type**: Domestic travel (no visa required)

## Weather Patterns

### Best Time to Visit

**North India** (Delhi, Jaipur, Agra, Varanasi)

- **Best**: October to March (15-25°C / 59-77°F)
- **Avoid**: April to June (extreme heat 40°C+ / 104°F+)
- **Monsoon**: July to September (humid, heavy rain)

**South India** (Bangalore, Chennai, Kochi, Trivandrum)

- **Best**: November to February (20-30°C / 68-86°F)
- **Pleasant**: Year-round in Bangalore (mild climate)
- **Monsoon**: June to September (West Coast), October to December (East Coast)

**West India** (Mumbai, Goa, Ahmedabad)

- **Best**: November to February (20-30°C / 68-86°F)
- **Beach Season**: October to March (Goa)
- **Monsoon**: June to September (heavy rain in Mumbai/Goa)

**East India** (Kolkata, Bhubaneswar)

- **Best**: October to March (15-28°C / 59-82°F)
- **Avoid**: April to June (hot and humid)
- **Monsoon**: June to September

## Cultural Tips

### Best Practices

- **Dress Code**: Modest clothing recommended (especially at religious sites)
- **Food**: Try local cuisine, but be cautious with street food initially
- **Language**: English widely spoken in cities, Hindi common in North
- **Currency**: Indian Rupee (INR), ₹1 ≈ $0.012
- **Tipping**: 10% in restaurants, ₹50-100 for services

### Must-Try Experiences

- **Food**: Biryani (Hyderabad), Street Food (Mumbai), Dosa (Bangalore)
- **Culture**: Temples, Forts, Palaces, Museums
- **Nature**: Backwaters (Kerala), Beaches (Goa), Mountains (nearby)
- **Shopping**: Textiles, Handicrafts, Jewelry, Spices

## Safety & Health

### Vaccinations

- **Recommended**: Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Routine vaccines
- **Consider**: Hepatitis B, Japanese Encephalitis (rural areas)
- **Malaria**: Prophylaxis for certain regions

### Safety Tips

- **Water**: Drink bottled water only
- **Food**: Eat at reputable restaurants
- **Transport**: Use official taxis/ride-sharing apps
- **Valuables**: Keep secure, be aware of surroundings

## Getting Around

### Within Cities

- **Metro**: Available in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata
- **Ride-Sharing**: Uber, Ola widely available
- **Auto-Rickshaws**: Convenient for short distances
- **Taxis**: Pre-paid or app-based recommended

### Between Cities

- **Flights**: Fastest option (1-3 hours)
- **Trains**: Extensive network, comfortable AC coaches
- **Buses**: Volvo/luxury buses available
- **Car Rental**: With driver recommended
